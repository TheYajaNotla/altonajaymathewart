02 / Praxis Statement

Alton Ajay Mathew (b. 2000) explores the dimensional and affective capacity of colour and form. Using charcoal-based underdrawings and overlays of high-contrast hues, the surface records structural gesture and human residue.

03 / Overview

Completing a Doctor of Medicine (M.D.) degree in Poland (2024), Alton weaves an interdisciplinary sensitivity to structural pathways and embodied visual flux into modern painterly abstraction.
